Amalaki Ekadashi

ફાગણ સુદ એકાદશી

✨ Significance

Associated with Lord Vishnu. Observing this fast brings immense spiritual merit, equal to donating a thousand cows.

🙏 Fasting Rules

Strict grain-free fast (Falahari). Break the fast (Parana) on Dwadashi during the auspicious Muhurat.
